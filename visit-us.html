VISIT THE DISTILLERY

Step into three generations of craft

Tucked into the village of Nikaia, just outside Larissa, our family distillery opens its doors to visitors who want to see — and taste — where every bottle begins.

AN AFTERNOON IN THESSALY

A visit to our distillery is not a tour in the usual sense.
There are no scripted speeches, no glass walkways, no gift shop bottlenecks.

What you'll find instead:
the copper still still warm from the morning, the smell of fermenting pomace in the back room, and a member of our family with a glass in hand, ready to share what we've been making for fifty years.

We keep it small. We keep it personal. The way it should be.

THE VISIT

TOUR

About 45 minutes

A walk through the distillery:

the fermentation vats, the copper stills, the bottling room. We explain what we do — and why we still do it this way.

TASTING

About 30 minutes

A guided tasting of our full range.

With anise, without. From the 200ml everyday to the 700ml feast. Paired with a small Greek meze plate.

TAKE HOME

Yours to keep

Every visit ends with a complimentary 200ml bottle of your choice — and the option to purchase any of our range directly from the distillery, at producer prices.
