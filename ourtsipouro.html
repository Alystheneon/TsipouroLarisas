Our Tsipouro

Two expressions, one copper still, the same family recipe.

40,000 Bottles / year
🍶 With Anise
PGI Thessaly
100% Pomace
🍷 Without Anise
Copper Stills
Traditional Greek Spirit
3 Generations
Family Recipe Since the 1970s

Our tsipouro is a pure pomace distillate of grapes from the vineyards of Thessaly. We distil it twice in small copper stills, in two expressions — clean and aromatic with anise, or pure and grape-forward without it. Around 40,000 bottles leave the distillery every year, no more.
