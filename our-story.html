WHAT WE STAND FOR

TRADITION

Methods refined over half a century. No shortcuts, no industrial scale, only the patience authentic tsipouro demands.

FAMILY

Three generations under one roof, sharing the same name on every bottle. What we make, we make together.

PLACE

Every grape comes from the Thessalian plains. The land shapes the spirit, and we wouldn't have it any other way.

Chapter 1

1978 — THE BEGINNING

In a small village outside Larissa, our grandfather built his first copper still. He had no marketing plan, no distribution network — only the grapes from his vineyard and the methods of his father before him.

What began as a small batch for the local taverna soon became something more. Word travelled. People came back. And so did their friends.

This is where the spirit of the plains was born.

Chapter 2

1990s — GROWTH

When our father took over, tsipouro was finding its place on Greek tables across the country. He expanded the distillery, refined the methods, and built relationships with tavernas and restaurants from Thessaloniki to Crete. But one thing never changed: every bottle came from our family's hands, made the way it always had been.

Tradition isn't something you keep. It's something you carry forward.

Chapter 3

2022 — NEW HORIZONS

Today, our family welcomes a new chapter. With respect for what came before, we're bringing the spirit of Thessaly to the world.

Modernised facilities, new international markets, a renewed presence wherever fine spirits are appreciated. But the heart of what we do remains where it began — in a small distillery in Nikaia, Larissa.

Three generations. One spirit. The plains, in every bottle.
